IDM 241 - Beta

Triggers

  • Hover on/off the photo
  • Hover on/off the "Add to cart" button
  • Click "Add to cart" button
  • Click "X" button

Rules

  • Hover On Photo
  • Drop shadow is applied to the card
  • Product name is underlined
  • Photo fades to the next image of the product after 1s
  • Hover Off Photo
  • Drop shadow and product name underline disappears
  • Photo transitions back to original image after 1s
  • Hover On Cart Button
  • Stroke remains aroung the button.
  • Fill of the button begins to fade to black
  • Black text inside fades to white
  • Hover Off Cart Button
  • Fill of the button begins to fade to white
  • White text inside fades to black
  • Stroke is visible again after button fades back to white
  • Click Cart Button
  • Modal slides in from left to right
  • Modal fades in while sliding
  • Click "X" Button
  • Modal slides out from right to left
  • Modal fades out while sliding

Loops & Modes

  • When the user clicks on the "Add to cart" button, a modal slides in from left to right while changing opactity. When the user clicks the "X" button on the modal, it slides out from right to left to close, restoring the original product screen.
